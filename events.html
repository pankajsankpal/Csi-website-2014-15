Ethical Hacking

Ethical Hacking event was conducted on 21st Sept.2014.Around 63 students form FE,SE and TE were present.Prof. Mahendra Mehra and Gaurav Tripathi(Microsoft Hackon Finalist) were the instructor for the event.

Topics covered in the Event was as follows:

  • Introduction to Network Security
  • Phishing and SQL injection
  • Concepts of Wireless Hacking
  • DDOS ,Trojan and Social Networking.

Web Development Workshop

Web Development Workshop was a 3 day event and was held in mid of odd semester of Academic Year 2014-15.Forty students of our college from FE,SE and TE were present. Prof. Mahendra Mehra was the Event Co-ordinator for the same. Rishabh jhunjhunwala(software Developer,Morgan Stanly) and Nilesh Dungarwal(IT analyst,Credit Suisse) were the speakers.

Topics covered in the Event was as follows:

  • concepts of HTML and JavaScript
  • Adding beautiful effects with bootstrap to a webpage
  • Develop a server side of website with PHP and MySQL

Java, RMI & Event Handling

Java workshop was held in even semester of the Academic Year 2012-13, two weeks before the cultural festival of our college, Euphoria. The Instructor for the same was Mr. Sanjay Makhija, who is an Industry-Training professional and owner of Mikado Solutions. The event was in collaboration with CESA-CRCE.

Content covered in the Event was as follows:

  • Object-Oriented Concepts
  • Core Java
  • GUI and Event Handling
  • Remote Method Invocation (RMI) and a Lab Exercise on the same.





Ethical Hacking & Cyber Security

  • Types of Security Threats
